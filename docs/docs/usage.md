# Usage

This section explains how to use SoundCore-Desktop once it is installed.

## Launch the app

After installation, launch SoundCore-Desktop. The app will appear in your system tray.

## Access controls

- Left-click the tray icon to open the control panel.
- Right-click the tray icon for the context menu.

## Configure your Soundcore device

1. Connect your Soundcore device to your computer.
2. Open the SoundCore-Desktop tray window.
3. Select the saved profile settings you want to apply.
4. Use the "Apply" or "Save" controls to persist the configuration.

## Restore settings automatically

Once a profile is configured, SoundCore-Desktop will detect the device connection event and restore your selected Soundcore settings automatically.

## Typical workflow

1. Start SoundCore-Desktop.
2. Pair or connect your Soundcore device.
3. Configure the preferred mode, ANC state, and equalizer profile.
4. Save the profile configuration.
5. Reconnect the device later; the app will reapply the saved profile.

## Notes

- The app is designed to work without the official Soundcore mobile app once your profile is configured.
- If the device is not detected, check Bluetooth connectivity and app permissions.
